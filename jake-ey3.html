Jake EY3

Twitter Instagram Discord Link

What is your artistic background/ origin story (education, journey, etc)

My name is Jake EY3 I am a 24yr old full time visual artist, I have been full time for 4 years I grew up making art and wanting to be an artist. When I was 3 years old I broke my femur bone and was in a full body cast for 9 months and as a young kid unable to run around and play I found my enjoyment spending time drawing and coloring and i really haven’t stopped creating since. I took some art classes in highschool and spent most of my time during lunches in breaks hiding away in the art room working on my projects. I won some highschool art competitions, and after highschool I tried taking a college painting class but I didnt align with what was being taught and decided to teach myself. Took a few normal 9-5 jobs after highschool for about 3 years before ending up at a Chris Dyer workshop. After that workshop I decided to take the leap and go full time, and have been living off of miracles ever since. Before NFTs I did alot of live painting and designs for collectible artifacts for all types of collectives, art producers and cool individuals who wish to use my art. Mostly revolving around the U.S. electric music festival culture. Collectible Enamel pins was one of my most collected items before stepping into NFTs in December 2021.

What mediums do you enjoy working in? (painting, Digital, drawing, printing, ceramics, woodworking etc)

I work in any medium I can. I do digital art, animation, painting, illustration, sculpting and casting.

What is the most interesting project you’ve worked on?

The project I have in front of me is always most interesting and gets my full attention.

Where do you find inspiration?

My art is inspired by countless things, ancient civilizations,modern civilizations, esoterics, spirituality, metaphysics,street art, reggae/Rasta, hip hop, timelessness, “aliens”, and the list goes on.

What motivates you?

EY3 always aim to bring art thatll uplift, empower,and/or educate. Showing people we create our reality. I grew up in In a “not so financially blessed” home, and had many people along the way tell me art isn’t a good career choice. So leading by example and showing others we can create our lives however we’d like is a constant motivation.

What do you do to get into the “artistic zone” (listen to music, meditate, eat good food)?

Herb and music, but the artistic zone is a constant state of being. I’m an art machine often forgetting to eat all day cuz I’m too “in the zone” .

What tips would you give to an artist brand new crypto and NFTs or art in general.

“Energy out is energy back.”

How important is creating a support system?

“Takes a tribe to raise an artist. No body is “self made”. Your support system is your symbiotic relationship with the world.”

If you could pick a useless super power, what would it be? (Ex: Crying glitter or having the ability to find your remote at home)

“Useless? Finding a remote sounds pretty useful  Let’s go with zero gravity while I’m sleeping so bugs don’t crawl on me….”

What are your plans for the future? Anything else you would like to share or promote?

Plans for the future would be to continue to grow my artistic abilities and sharpen my tool of creating what I see in my minds EY3, and change the world with visual enhancements that expand human thoughts and create art that’ll be push the world to better days. Cuz WHY NOT aim Big! Thanks for reading  ONE love
